Profile

Anshu Ravi is a passionate game designer, visual effects artist, and interactive media student inspired to create immersive experiences. She is proficient in a variety of software tools aiding her practice. This includes Blender for 3D modeling, animation, and rendering, alongside mastery of the Adobe Suite for motion graphics, and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for web development. Anshu’s goal is to seamlessly blend creativity with technical finesse and create experiences to transport audiences into immersive worlds. She is currently exploring opportunities in game design, vfx, web development, and interaction design.
