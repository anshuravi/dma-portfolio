Project Overview

Echoes of Fear is a choose-your-own-adventure visual novel that explores the realms of fear through interactive storytelling. The game allows players to navigate through different fears, making choices that affect the outcome of the story. The project was made for San Jose State University's Fall 2024 Digital Media Art Exhibition, Press Play.

Artist Statement

In a clouded existential dread, I sit in the darkness of humanity, until I hear a calling to a land beyond our own, a land of fantasy. My artistic practice bridges this abyss between the real world and my inner fantastical one. I use the realms of interactive technology to spin tales of these fantastical lands to remember that there is still hope. The struggles I face in existence fall down the chasm with me as only in these enchanted tales do I rise valiantly.

Portal into Echoes of Fear, a choose your own adventure story exploring my battles with fear. Follow the spirit of fear down the rabbit hole, confronting obstacles such as judgment, failure, change, and more. My desire is that as I have, many will immerse themselves within the story and confront their own reality. As I do, they will assume victory as their own hero dispels the darkness before the epilogue.

Process

The milestones of completing this project included:

  1. Plan out the story branches in Twine
  2. Input the story into Visual Studio Code with JavaScript for interactions, HTML for structure and CSS for styling
  3. Draw out illustrations with Autodesk Sketchbook and input them
  4. Transfer everything to a USB and prepare for showcase setup
  5. Showcase

Technologies Used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Autodesk Sketchbook
